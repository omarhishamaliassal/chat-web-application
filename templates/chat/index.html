About ChatSafe Team

Our team of four students from Misr International University has created an app to make chatting safer and easier for everyone. We've put a lot of effort into designing it to be user-friendly and secure. Our goal is to change how people communicate online, making it more comfortable and private for everyone. We hope our app will set a new standard for chatting online, making it simpler and safer for all users.
